Hell guard

Burning with the urge of vengeance, Wan unleashes his third form. His eyes glow orange with the fresh pyro energy streaming out of his body. Wan captures the fires from hell and summons them to the opponents in front of him. (One or more scythes are required for him to use this 'hell guard' form properly.) Wan's stamina is enhanced. However, at this stage, it is harder to increase the rage points. The next stage can only be unlocked via specific achievements.
