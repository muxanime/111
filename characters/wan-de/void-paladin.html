Void paladin

The 'void paladin' stage allows Wan De to lay assault to opponents by harnessing the power of the void element. Speed and strength attributes are enhanced due to the rapidly switching energy orbs fusing with Wan's soul. Darkness fills all his targets' hearts, particularly effective against photomancers. Once he accumulates more than 20000 points worth of rage, he can transform to the 'hell guard' stage. A scythe is required for the transformation.


Element : voidmancy


Stage type : intermediate
